› Apothecary Labels

Livev0.16.xWeb ToolVanilla JS

A browser-based label designer for Celtic-styled apothecary jars. Type a herb name and a finished label assembles itself: parchment, gold trim, runes, and botanical art.

details

The lookup carries nearly two hundred herbs with curated Celtic associations. Pick one and the label auto-fills with a parchment background, gold trim, Elder Futhark runes, Celtic symbols, and a line-art botanical illustration from a built-in library. Five label sizes from 1.5 by 0.75 inches up to 6 by 3 share their proportions exactly.

An optional back label carries a full description, historic uses, notes, and pairings, and front and back print together on a single 8.5 by 11 sheet. Accent colors and per-element color and glow overrides are exposed on everything, and work saves to localStorage.

The current focus is symbol and label polish.
