› Brackish Rising

Pre-MVPv0.57.xGodot 4.6RTS

A grim-dark survival RTS in true 2:1 isometric, sibling to Here Be Hordes but its own world. The deep ocean climbed out of the trenches a hundred and sixty years ago and never receded.

details

You command a fortified Lantern outpost on the inland edge, holding the wall against echo-hunting parasites that wear what is left of the people they took. The tech ceiling is industrial-medieval crossed with WWI-naval: lamps, dredges, riveted armor, and sonar.

The central mechanic is Sonic Pressure. Anything that hums draws the parasites in, so quiet colonies live and loud ones die. Engagements are slower and heavier than in Hordes: smaller groups of high-HP enemies, longer fights, and every unit lost is a real loss. Coral encroaches inland year by year, and optional sorties push the perimeter back, but survival is the only victory condition.

Brackish Rising forked from an alternate direction for Hordes and now stands alone. The two are siblings and do not cross-contaminate.

design
