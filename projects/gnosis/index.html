A living wiki for the Elder Domain, my homebrew D&D world. Every character, place, faction, god, item, shop, and session is one linked page.

details

The source of truth is a plain-markdown vault: one file per entity with structured frontmatter and wiki-style links, version-controlled like code. The site renders it as a browsable wiki with backlinks, per-type indexes, search, and an interactive pinned world map - and the vault stays portable to any editor, forever.

Built-in seeded name generators cover people across five cultures, settlements, taverns, shops with generated keepers, and factions - the random-generator workflow, but offline, deterministic, and one keypress away while writing.

The whole site sits behind a DM gate: players see nothing without a login, and spoiler entities carry a secret flag that keeps them out of any future player-facing view by construction. Cloudflare Worker, zero-framework renderer.
